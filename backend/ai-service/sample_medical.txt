Medical Conditions Overview:

1. Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus
   - Chronic metabolic disorder affecting glucose regulation
   - Requires regular monitoring of blood sugar levels
   - Treatment includes lifestyle modifications and medication

2. Essential Hypertension
   - Persistent elevation of blood pressure
   - Risk factor for cardiovascular disease
   - Management includes diet, exercise, and antihypertensive medications

3. Bronchial Asthma
   - Chronic inflammatory airway disease
   - Characterized by recurrent breathing difficulties
   - Treated with bronchodilators and corticosteroids

Treatment Protocols:

- Regular health checkups every 3-6 months
- Medication adherence is critical
- Lifestyle modifications: balanced diet, regular exercise
- Stress management techniques
- Adequate sleep and hydration

Emergency Warning Signs:

Seek immediate medical attention if experiencing:
- Severe chest pain or pressure
- Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
- Sudden severe headache
- Loss of consciousness
- Uncontrolled bleeding

Preventive Care:

- Annual health screenings
- Vaccination as per schedule
- Regular physical activity (30 minutes daily)
- Balanced nutrition with fruits and vegetables
- Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
